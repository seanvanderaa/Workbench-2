Fact Check Project Title
Created: Sept. 1, 2025

CLAIM

Is there a secure process to track Arizona’s mail-in ballots?

VERDICT

Mostly True

CONFIDENCE

1

SUMMARIES

Arizona provides official state and county tracking portals and notifications. Security is strong but relies on standard web authentication, vendor systems, and county practices.

By official counts, Bloomberg’s New Housing Marketplace Plan financed about 165,000 affordable homes [nyc.gov]. De Blasio’s Housing New York surpassed 200,000 financed homes by 2021 [nyc.gov]. Combined, that’s well over 350,000. Adams announced a record 2023 with roughly 27,000 “starts” [nyc.gov], but his cumulative production since 2022 is far below the Bloomberg+de Blasio total. Independent tallies confirm predecessors together financed more than 365,000 units, dwarfing Adams’s to date [cbcny.org]. Metrics matter—press releases often cite “starts,” while the Mayor’s Management Report tracks both starts and completions [nyc.gov]. Under any common metric and timeframe, there’s no basis for saying Adams has built more than both combined.

VERIFIED FACTS

Fact

Arizona’s Secretary of State offers an official ballot-by-mail status lookup/tracking portal.

Clarification

State portal lets voters check ballot mailed, received, signature verification, acceptance.
